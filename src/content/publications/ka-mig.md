This work augments masked image generation with explicit token-level priors, including co-occurrence, semantic similarity, and position-token incompatibility graphs. Those priors are injected through a graph-aware encoder and lightweight fusion module, improving generation quality on class-conditional ImageNet tasks.

It fits the broader theme of using structured prior information to compensate for weaknesses in purely data-driven token sequence learning.
